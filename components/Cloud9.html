Cloud9

CLoud9 has a side tool nav bar. I have not seen this very often so thought I would give it a try nav bars are great for any web site. They allow the user to navigate thi site. Having a side nav bar gives the page an open feel usually all the important content is in the "center stage" of a website allowing clear visibility. So it's a good idea to have the nav bar out of the way but still accessible.
